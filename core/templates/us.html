What do I need to rent a car?
To rent a car on DriveToday you must have an account fisrt. Then each person who intends to drive must have a valid driver's license and a valid means of payment (generally via credit cash). All of these items must be submitted on the day the car will be picked up.

Can I rent a car without a reservation?
Yes, as long as a vehicle is available in the station; however, we recommend making a reservation because no vehicle may be available during busy periods.
